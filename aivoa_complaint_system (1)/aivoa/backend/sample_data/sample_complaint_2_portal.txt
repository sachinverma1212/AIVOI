CUSTOMER COMPLAINT REPORT
Reference: ICC-2026-00154
Submitted via: Customer Portal
Customer: ABC Formulations Ltd.

Product Name: Metformin Hydrochloride API
Product Strength/Grade: IP/BP
Batch / Lot Number: CHG260712A
Manufacturing Date: 25 June 2026
Expiry Date: Not Provided
Affected Quantity: 50 kg (1 HDPE Drum)

Complaint Category: Foreign Matter Contamination

Complaint Description:
During incoming quality inspection at our formulation facility, our QC team
identified multiple dark foreign particles inside one sealed HDPE drum of
Metformin Hydrochloride API. The drum exterior showed no visible external
damage, and the tamper-evident seal was unbroken at the time of opening. The
particles are dark grey/black, irregular in shape, and estimated at 1-3mm in
size. The material has been quarantined pending investigation. We request a
formal root cause investigation and CAPA, along with confirmation of whether
any other drums from the same batch are affected.

Reported by: QC Manager, ABC Formulations Ltd.
Date of Complaint: 2026-07-18
